About This Journey

I'm an average mountaineer with an extraordinary passion for the peaks. Starting with local climbs in the Northeast, I've expanded my horizons to tackle some of North America's most challenging summits. Every mountain tells a story—of preparation, perseverance, fear, and triumph.

This site documents my past achievements, upcoming adventures, and the knowledge I've gained along the way. Whether you're a seasoned alpinist or just considering your first peak, there's something here for you. Mountain climbing isn't about being the best—it's about being better than you were yesterday and pushing your personal limits in the most beautiful landscapes on Earth.

From the technical rock of Mt. Hood to the altitude of the Bolivian Andes, join me as I pursue the summits and share the insights that come from climbing them.
