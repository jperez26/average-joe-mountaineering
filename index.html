About me

I'm a damned noob but tis okay because everyone started off as a beginer. Starting with climbs in the Northeast on Washington, I really just want to see what I am capable of and what it will take for me to have to turn around.

This site documents my past summits, upcoming adventures, and the knowledge I've gained along the way having to start as a guy who has only ever went hiking in OHIO before and never stepping foot on a moutain to plans to summit Rainier within the same year. Whether you're a seasoned alpinist laughing at me or just considering your first peak, there's something here for you. Mountain climbing isn't about being the best its about pushing your personal limits in the most beautiful landscapes god has gifted us with on this Earth.

From the first apline start jitters of Mt. Hood to the altitude of the Bolivian Andes, join me as I pursue the summits and share the insights that come from climbing them, solo for as much as I can, although as I learn about crevases that seems to get MUCH harder the higher you climb.
